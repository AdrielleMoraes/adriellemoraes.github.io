SCIENCE

At moment, I am enrolled as a master by Research degree in Software Engineering at Athlone Institute of Technology. This research is fully funded by Science Foundation Ireland in partnership with ADAPT centre and AIT. This project aims to develop an immersive application to evaluate the Quality of Experience (QoE) of a listener performing sound localisation in a virtual reality environment
